ABOUT US

We're inspired by athletes. From the very first track spikes Adi Dassler made in the 1920s to today's technological design innovations. Our sneakers and apparel are worn by world-record winners, but it's just as important that road runners, weekend hikers, rec league heroes and fitness enthusiasts. From adidas Boost, the game-changing cushioning of Adizero Adios Pro running shoes to Terrex outdoor wear with unbeatable touch, we're always iterating, innovating and improving.

The 3-Stripes have appeared on medal stands all around the world, but the influence that extends far beyond the field of play. adidas sneakers have become legends in their own right. Celebrated by performers, skaters, artists and all the other change-makers who move the world forward with their vision and uncompromising style. We've made our mark with legends like the adidas Superstar, the Stan Smith and the Samba, shoes that began as tools for sport and became icons of culture, hitting the streets and leaving an indelible impression on the culture.

adidas Originals apparel is a stylish complement to our lifestyle shoes. Our design ethos is rooted in authentic sportswear. We pull from the archives to put a modern spin on classics like the Firebird track suit. Luxe materials, sophisticated silhouettes and unexpected details elevate heritage streetwear. adidas Originals apparel transcend sport while at the same time never losing sight of our heritage. Because for the past 70 years and has always been, that through sport we have the power to change lives.
